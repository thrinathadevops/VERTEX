Technical interviewing is broken. Senior engineers waste dozens of hours a week conducting repetitive preliminary screens, while candidates suffer through anxiety-inducing, non-standardized interrogations.

Enter **AI-Powered Technical Interviews**.

## Eliminating Human Bottlenecks
By leveraging advanced LLMs, companies can deploy automated, conversational technical assessments that deeply evaluate a candidate's actual engineering logic rather than just memorized LeetCode syntax.

1. **Unbiased Evaluation:** The AI does not care what school the candidate went to or what their voice sounds like. It rigorously grades exactly what they code.
2. **Infinite Scalability:** You can screen 5,000 candidates simultaneously over the weekend, delivering a ranked list of the top 10 to your hiring managers by Monday morning.
3. **Candidate Practice:** Our platform allows candidates to take "Mock Interviews" against the AI to practice and receive instantaneous, constructive feedback on their system design answers.

AI is not here to replace the hiring manager; it is here to replace the grueling technical screen.
